The "Magic Markup" = Template Variables/Placeholders
The term you're looking for is "template variables" or "placeholders" - the process of replacing them is called "variable interpolation" or "template substitution".
Common syntax patterns:
* {{Name}} - Handlebars/Mustache style (very popular)
* {Name} - Simple braces (cleaner, less visual clutter)
* ${Name} - JavaScript template literal style
* [[Name]] - Double bracket style
