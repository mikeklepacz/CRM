Polkadog is a Boston-based company founded in 2002 by Deborah Suchman and Rob Van Sickle. It specializes in handcrafted, all-natural pet treats for dogs and cats, inspired by their rescue dog, Pearl. The company emphasizes sustainable sourcing and eco-friendly practices, starting as a small bakery and growing into a global brand with wholesale distribution across the US, Canada, and South Korea.

Polkadog operates eight retail stores in the Boston area and an industrial kitchen at Boston Fish Pier. The company employs around 95-100 people and generates approximately $8 million in revenue. Its product lineup includes popular items like Cod Skins, Clam Chowda, and Green Monstah, all made from locally sourced ingredients. Polkadog focuses on healthy, allergy-friendly options, with treats made fresh daily and packaged for retail and wholesale. The company promotes community ties and healthy pet nutrition, making its products available in independent pet stores, natural food shops, and grocery stores worldwide."
