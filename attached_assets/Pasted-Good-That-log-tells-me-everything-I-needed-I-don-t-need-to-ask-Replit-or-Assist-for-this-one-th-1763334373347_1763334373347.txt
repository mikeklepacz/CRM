Why this breaks the scheduler

candidate is in UTC.

addDays(candidate, 1) advances the day in UTC, NOT in the admin’s timezone.

So you get:
	•	Admin day: based on adminTimezone
	•	Recipient day: based on recipientTimezone
	•	Loop day increment: based on UTC

Three different calendars are being mixed together.
This absolutely guarantees that the windows land on different days, even when they should overlap.

It results in a scheduler that thinks it’s rolling from Wed → Thu, but the recipient window stays on Wednesday, so overlap = false.

This is why even with perfect business hours and perfect timezones it still dies.

	•	The rolling day always stays aligned with the admin’s calendar.
