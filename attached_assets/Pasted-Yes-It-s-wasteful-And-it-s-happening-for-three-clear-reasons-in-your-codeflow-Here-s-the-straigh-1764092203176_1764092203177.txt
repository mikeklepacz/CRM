Before tuning anything

Tell me one thing so we solve this correctly:

Do you want these background systems to run strictly on schedule (fixed intervals), or do you want them to be event-driven (run only when input changes)?

Event-driven = efficient, but requires more wiring (signals, triggers, DB listeners).

This one choice determines the right architecture path.
