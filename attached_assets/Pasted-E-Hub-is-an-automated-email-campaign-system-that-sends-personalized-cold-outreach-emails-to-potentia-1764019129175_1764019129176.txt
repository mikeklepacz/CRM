E-Hub is an automated email campaign system that sends personalized cold outreach emails to potential customers (cannabis dispensaries) using AI to write every message. Here's what it does and its key features:

Core Purpose
Instead of manually writing hundreds of similar emails, E-Hub automates the entire process while maintaining personalization through AI-generated content for each recipient.

Key Features
1. Campaign Strategy Planning
Chat with AI to define campaign goals, messaging, tone, and target audience
AI stores this strategy as the blueprint for all emails in the sequence
Set step delays (how many days between Email 1, Email 2, Email 3, etc.)
2. Recipient Management
Import contacts from Google Sheets (Store Database or Commission Tracker)
Track contact status: never contacted, contacted, in sequence, replied, bounced
View all contacts across sequences with filtering and search
Blacklist emails to exclude from enrollment
3. AI Email Generation
Each email is uniquely written by AI (no templates)
Uses your campaign strategy + recipient context (name, business info, timezone)
Generates both subject lines and HTML body content
Follow-ups reference previous emails naturally
Uses specialized "Aligner" AI assistant with knowledge base
4. Smart Scheduling (Matrix2 System)
Respects recipient business hours and timezones
Spreads emails throughout the day with random jitter to avoid spam flags
Enforces daily email limits (default 150/day)
Skips weekends if configured
Three-tier priority: Manual Follow-Ups → Active Follow-Ups → Cold Outreach
5. Reply Detection
Auto-detects Gmail replies and stops sending to those recipients
Marks recipients as "replied" to prevent further emails
Scanner tool can detect manual Gmail draft sends and enroll them in follow-up sequences
6. Queue Management
View upcoming emails scheduled across next 3 days
Color-coded status: Green (sent), Blue (scheduled), Gray (open slots), Red (overdue)
Actions: pause recipient, skip step, send now, delay by X hours, remove from sequence
Manual queue rebuild to apply new jitter settings
7. Campaign Controls
Activate/pause sequences
View sent history with filtering
Synthetic email preview (test how emails will look)
Test email sending to verify templates
8. Integration
Gmail OAuth for sending
Google Sheets for contact data
Commission Tracker auto-updates to "Emailed" status
Timezone detection from store locations
Workflow Example
Create sequence: "Cold Outreach Q1 2025"
Chat with AI about campaign strategy
Set delays: [0 days, 3 days, 7 days] = Email 1 immediately, Email 2 after 3 days, Email 3 after 7 more days
Import 100 contacts from Google Sheets
Activate sequence
System automatically generates unique emails for each person and sends at optimal times
If someone replies, system stops sending to them
View progress in Queue tab
The system handles all the complexity of timing, personalization, and reply detection automatically.
