Here’s the compact bullet summary, stripped to essentials but complete.

PRODUCT: Email Exchange (Cold Outreach Engine + CRM Sync)
Purpose: Controlled cold outreach at scale with automated follow-ups, reply detection, and CRM sync. Admin-only tool.

Data Intake
	•	Pulls from Google Sheets “Store Database” → Name (A), Email (K), Hours (N), Sales Summary (P).
	•	Only uses externally validated emails.
	•	Emails sent only 1 hour after local opening time, not after 2pm, no weekends/holidays.

AI Personalization
	•	OPENAI generates unique email bodies using: name, email, sales summary.
	•	Prompt-injection field available to tune tone, structure, and rules.
	•	Keyword bin can be added for extra context.

Follow-Up Sequencer
	•	Preloaded plan: Day 1, 3, 7, 15, 31, then monthly.
	•	Adjustable schedule.
	•	Threaded replies.
	•	Stops sequence instantly when a reply arrives (IMAP check).

Variant Engine
	•	Subject_bin + Body_bin with placeholders.
	•	Randomized selection or AI-generated subjects.
	•	Light variations to keep messages unique.

Send Queue
	•	Sends 1 email every X–Y minutes (randomized).
	•	Per-sender identity pacing.
	•	No batch blasts.

Personalization Layer
	•	Each message injects the shop’s unique Sales Summary.
	•	Mentions discovering them on Leafly.
	•	Ensures hand-written feel.

Reply Detection
	•	IMAP worker checks inbox hourly (3pm–midnight Warsaw).
	•	On reply: sequence halts, CRM updated, admin notified.

Auto Follow-Up
	•	If no reply, next step triggers based on schedule.
	•	Uses same thread to boost deliverability.

CRM Sync
	•	Step 1 sets Commission Tracker “Status (H)” → “Contacted.”
	•	Logs timestamp.
	•	Manual outreach by humans also syncs back to prevent duplicates.

Agent Awareness
	•	System stores which agent contacted each shop.
	•	No double outreach across agents.

A/B Testing
	•	Based only on reply rate (no opens).
	•	Logs which subject/body variant triggered a response.

Deliverability Protection
	•	No tracking pixels, no heavy images.
	•	Warm-up curve; randomized timing.
	•	Multiple sending identities (mike@, mike1@, etc.).
	•	Bounce detection; removes hard bounces.

Email Identity
	•	System can login using different sending accounts independent of browser/CRM session.

Dashboard
	•	Shows: validated emails, contacted status, last email sent, next scheduled send, assigned variant, reply state.
	•	Global stats: sends per day, per-identity limits, bounce metrics, domain health.
