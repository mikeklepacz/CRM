Looking at the error "No legal send slot found within 14 days" and the console logs, I can see the issue is in the Matrix Scheduler (matrixScheduler.ts).

The error occurs when you try to import recipients into a sequence. The scheduler is trying to find a valid time slot that satisfies all these constraints:

Admin sending window (sendingHoursStart to sendingHoursEnd)
Recipient business hours (when the store is open)
Weekend skipping (if enabled)
Client window constraints (clientWindowStartOffset and clientWindowEndHour)
The scheduler searches for 14 days but can't find any overlapping time that satisfies all constraints.

Why this happens:

Looking at the code in matrixScheduler.ts around line 220, the scheduler calculates the overlap between:

Admin's sending window (in their timezone)
Recipient's business hours (in their timezone)
Maximum send time constraint (clientWindowEndHour, default 2pm recipient time)
If there's no overlap for 14 consecutive days, it throws this error.

Most likely causes:

Admin timezone vs recipient timezone conflict - The admin's sending hours (in their timezone) don't overlap with the recipient's business hours (in their timezone) after converting both to UTC
clientWindowEndHour too restrictive - Default is 14:00 (2pm) recipient local time, which may be cutting off valid slots
Weekend skipping - If enabled and the next 14 days have mostly weekends or the recipient is closed weekdays
