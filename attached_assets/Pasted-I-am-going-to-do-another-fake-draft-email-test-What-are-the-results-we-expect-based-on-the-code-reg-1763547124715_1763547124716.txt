Based on the code analysis, here's what should happen when you send a draft email from Store Details:

Expected Flow
1. Email Preview Component (client/src/components/email-preview.tsx)
When you click "Create Gmail Draft" or use mailto:

Calls POST /api/gmail/create-draft with to, subject, body, and clientLink
Then calls POST /api/email-drafts to enroll in Manual Follow-Ups
2. Manual Follow-Ups Enrollment (server/routes.ts)
The POST /api/email-drafts endpoint should:

Find or create the "Manual Follow-Ups" sequence (system sequence)
Create a new recipient record at Step 1 (not Step 0) with these fields:
email, name, salesSummary, businessHours, link, state, timezone
currentStep: 1
status: 'in_sequence'
lastStepSentAt: new Date() (timestamp of the draft send)
Create a message record in sequence_recipient_messages with:
stepNumber: 1
subject, body
sentAt: new Date()
gmailMessageId (if available from Gmail API)
3. What Happens in E-Hub
All Contacts Tab
The contact should appear with:
neverContacted: false (because they're now in a sequence)
inSequence: true
sequenceNames: ['Manual Follow-Ups']
Recipients Tab
If you select "Manual Follow-Ups" sequence, the contact appears
Shows as Step 1 (already sent)
Status should be in_sequence
Queue Tab
Contact will NOT appear in active queue immediately
They need to wait for the step delay before appearing
The "Manual Follow-Ups" sequence has stepDelays configured in the sequence settings
Next send will be scheduled based on: lastStepSentAt + stepDelays[1] (the delay for Step 2)
4. Matrix2 Slot Assignment
Later, when the delay period expires:

slotAssigner.ts will find this recipient as eligible
Assigns them to an open slot in daily_send_slots
Respects their timezone and business hours
Email gets sent automatically when slot time arrives
Key Architecture Points
Step Numbering: Manual drafts go to Step 1, NOT Step 0 (this prevents accidental instant-sends)
AI Follow-ups: First AI-generated follow-up happens at Step 2 after the configured delay
Scanner Integration: The Reply Scanner can also enroll contacts at Step 1 (same as manual drafts)
Priority: Manual Follow-Ups recipients at Step 2+ get Tier 1 priority in slot assignment
What You Should See
After sending a test draft:

✅ Gmail draft created (or mailto opened)
✅ Contact appears in E-Hub → All Contacts → "In Sequence" filter
✅ Contact appears in E-Hub → Sequences → "Manual Follow-Ups" row
✅ Contact appears in E-Hub → Recipients tab (when Manual Follow-Ups selected)
⏳ Contact appears in Queue tab after delay period expires
🔄 Automated AI follow-up sends at Step 2 when slot time arrives
The critical check is: Does the contact appear in All Contacts with inSequence: true? That confirms enrollment worked.
