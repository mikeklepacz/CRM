The Apollo Model Fix
You need a unified scheduler that treats both constraints as equally binding:

Global Constraint: Queue position + rate limiting + admin window
Recipient Constraint: Local business hours + timezone + client delivery window

The scheduler must find the first available timestamp where:

It's after the queue tail + minimum spacing
It falls within admin sending hours (UTC)
It falls within recipient's legal delivery window (local time)
Weekends are skipped if configured
If no intersection exists today, roll to next valid day and repeat.
